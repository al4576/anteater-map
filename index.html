About Find An Anteater

This map helps people find places to see anteaters around the world. All submissions go through manual review before anything on the map is changed.

Submission Guidelines

Notes — Great for individual anteater names, fun facts, and where to find them within the park.

Encounters — Only listed if you can directly feed or pet the anteater. Shows and appearances do not count.

Encounter Details — Pricing, scheduling, booking info, age limits, and other practical details.

We're also looking to update the ant icon on the map — if you have ideas or design skills, get in touch!

Feedback of any kind is always welcome.
